🚀 Smart Elevator System

Making elevators faster, smarter, and more efficient

Problem Statement

Current elevator systems lack a reliable, fail-safe, and privacy-preserving mechanism to confirm whether passengers are actually waiting before stopping at a floor. This leads to unnecessary stops, inefficient movement, and increased energy consumption. As a result, users experience longer waiting times and reduced service quality. Additionally, these inefficiencies can create accessibility issues for individuals who depend on fast and reliable elevator services.
