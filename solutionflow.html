🚀 ElevateX

Making elevators faster, smarter, and more efficient

Solution Flow

1

Button Press

User presses the elevator call button.

⬇
2

Zone Detection

IR sensors create a smart waiting zone near the elevator.

⬇
3

Human Detection

System detects if a person is present inside the zone.

⬇
4

Multi-Frame Confirmation

Multiple frame checks ensure accurate detection.

⬇
5

Final Decision

✔ Person present → Elevator stops ❌ No person → Elevator skips floor

✨ Outcome

Reduced energy waste, faster service, improved efficiency, and better accessibility for all users.
